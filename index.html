Qualitative Accomplishments

  • Once I understand something, truly understand it, my ability to use it is increased a hundredfold. At the same time, though my memory is sometimes good enough to replicate things, too often things that I don't understand get the better of me.
  • People tend to like me, a great share of the time. Though no one can be liked by all they meet, I come close. It often results in people trusting me, which helps both parties when collaboration is involved. The downside to this magnetism is that I am sometimes distracting to others, because it is easy to talk about nearly anything. Since people feel comfortable around me, the barriers that normally dictate formality are often disregarded in entirety.
  • I'm very logic based. As long as there is an order, some logic inherent in the things I take part in, I've a fighting chance to understand. Though, at the same time, it means that true chaos can throw me off my groove.

Whole Brain Paragraph

I'm more inclined towards logic than creativity. Inspiration comes only when it wants, but perspiration is always within our grasp. I've always liked things that can be quantified, more so than that whose value is subjective. It is important to remember, though, that both sides are necessary to make the best technology.

My second week has been somewhat harder than the first. PHP has been rather confusing, and I've not yet gotten the hang of it. Once again, I am thankful for my wise, and kind classmates. Whenever I feel lost, I know I can ask them for help, and we'll both be better off for it. This week's greatest challenge has been trying to learn so much knowledge in such a short time. Though it's been difficult, I relish the challenge.
